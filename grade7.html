📘 Chapter 1: Integers

Lesson Summary

Integers are whole numbers that can be positive, negative, or zero. Operations with integers include addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, powers, mixed operations (PEMDAS), and arranging integers in order.

8) Long Expressions (PEMDAS)

Solved Examples

  1. (2 + 3) × (4 - 1)² = (5 × 3²) = 45
  2. (-4 + 6)³ ÷ 2 = (2³ ÷ 2) = 4
  3. ((3 × 2) - 5)² = (6 - 5)² = 1
  4. (10 - (3 + 2)) × (-2) = (10 - 5) × (-2) = -10
  5. ((-2)² + (5 - 3)³) = (4 + 8) = 12
  6. (12 ÷ (2 × 3)) + 7 = (12 ÷ 6) + 7 = 9
  7. ((-3 + 9) ÷ 3)² = (6 ÷ 3)² = 2² = 4
  8. (20 - (4²)) ÷ (-2) = (20 - 16) ÷ (-2) = 4 ÷ (-2) = -2
  9. ((2 + 5)³ - 100) ÷ 5 = (343 - 100) ÷ 5 = 243 ÷ 5 = 48.6
  10. (-1 + (2 × 3))² = (-1 + 6)² = 5² = 25

Practice Examples

  2. (-5 + 10)³ ÷ 5 25
  3. ((8 ÷ 2) + 3)² 49
  4. (15 - (4 × 2)) × (-3) -9

  6. (20 ÷ (5 + 5)) × 4 8
