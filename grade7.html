📘 Chapter 1: Integers

Lesson Summary

Integers are whole numbers that can be positive, negative, or zero. Operations with integers include addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, powers, mixed operations (PEMDAS), and arranging integers in order.

8) Long Expressions (PEMDAS)

Solved Examples

  1. (2 + 3) × (4 - 1)² = (5 × 3²) = 45
  2. (-4 + 6)³ ÷ 2 = (2³ ÷ 2) = 4
  3. ((3 × 2) - 5)² = (6 - 5)² = 1
  4. (10 - (3 + 2)) × (-2) = (10 - 5) × (-2) = -10
  5. ((-2)² + (5 - 3)³) = (4 + 8) = 12
  6. (12 ÷ (2 × 3)) + 7 = (12 ÷ 6) + 7 = 9
  7. ((-3 + 9) ÷ 3)² = (6 ÷ 3)² = 2² = 4
  8. (20 - (4²)) ÷ (-2) = (20 - 16) ÷ (-2) = 4 ÷ (-2) = -2
  9. ((2 + 5)³ - 100) ÷ 5 = (343 - 100) ÷ 5 = 243 ÷ 5 = 48.6
  10. (-1 + (2 × 3))² = (-1 + 6)² = 5² = 25

📘 Chapter 2: Fractions

Lesson Summary

Addition: Make denominators equal, then add numerators.

Subtraction: Same as addition, but subtract numerators.

Multiplication: Multiply numerators and denominators, then simplify.

Division: Multiply the first fraction by the reciprocal of the second.
