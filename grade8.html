Grade 8 - Algebraic Expressions (Expand and Reduce)

Addition and subtraction:
- To add or subtract they must have the "same terms".
Examples:
2x + 3x = 5x
5x² - x² = 4x²
8xy + 3xy = 11xy
...

Multiplication and division:
- They must have "like terms".
Examples:
x × x² = x³
(2x²)(3x³) = 6x⁵
y³ × y⁵ = y⁸
...

Expanding and reducing:
Remarkable identities:
(a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b²
(a - b)² = a² - 2ab + b²
(a + b)(a - b) = a² - b²

Examples:
(x + 1)² = x² + 2x + 1
(2x - 3)² = 4x² - 12x + 9
(x + 3)(x - 3) = x² - 9
...

Distribution:
Multiplication is distributive over addition or subtraction.
Examples:
3x(x + 2) = 3x² + 6x
2x(x - 3) = 2x² - 6x
(x + 1)(x - 2) = x² - x - 2
...
