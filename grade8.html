📘 Chapter 1: Powers

Summary

The power of a number shows how many times the number is multiplied by itself. Example: 23 = 2 × 2 × 2 = 8

  • am × an = am+n
  • am ÷ an = am-n
  • (am)n = am×n
  • (ab)n = an × bn
  • a0 = 1 (for a ≠ 0)
  • a-n = 1 / an
